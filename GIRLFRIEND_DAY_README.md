# Girlfriend Day Feature - August 1st Special

## Overview

A beautiful, romantic feature created specifically for August 1st (Girlfriend Day) to make your girlfriend Sha feel incredibly loved and special.

## Features

### 🎉 Welcome Message

-   Automatically shows on August 1st when the app opens
-   Beautiful animated welcome with crown and sparkles
-   Personalized greeting for Sha

### 💝 Interactive Love Journey

-   5 beautiful steps with romantic messages
-   Each step has a unique icon and color theme
-   Interactive heart button that counts clicks
-   Special messages appear at 10 and 100 heart clicks

### ⏰ Time-Based Messages

-   Messages change based on time of day:
    -   Morning (5 AM - 12 PM): "Good morning, my beautiful Sha!"
    -   Afternoon (12 PM - 5 PM): "Good afternoon, my love!"
    -   Evening (5 PM - 9 PM): "Good evening, my darling!"
    -   Night (9 PM - 5 AM): "Good night, my precious Sha!"

### 🎊 Special Effects

-   Floating hearts animation in the background
-   Confetti animation when reaching the final surprise
-   Glowing effects and smooth transitions
-   Achievement badge for reaching 100 heart clicks

### 💖 Final Surprise

-   Beautiful love letter with personal message
-   Animated hearts display
-   Countdown to next special day
-   Your signature "Forever yours, Riza"

### 🎨 Visual Design

-   Romantic gradient backgrounds
-   Glassmorphism effects with backdrop blur
-   Responsive design for all devices
-   Smooth animations and hover effects

## How It Works

1. **Automatic Detection**: The app automatically detects when it's August 1st
2. **Welcome Screen**: Shows a special welcome message for Sha
3. **Step-by-Step Journey**: 5 romantic steps with interactive elements
4. **Heart Counter**: Click the heart button to show love
5. **Special Achievements**: Unlock special messages at 10 and 100 clicks
6. **Final Surprise**: Beautiful ending with confetti and love letter
